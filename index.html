About Me.


I'm Cherno Basiru Jallow nickname CBJ. A tech geek, programmer, researcher and Youtuber studying at Nusrat Senior Secondary School.
I run CBJtech youtube channel, where i share my knowledge on programming, AI and tech in general.
From a young age, I.ve always been interested in technology and how it can be used to make the world a better place. This passion led me to start learning about web development, and I soon realized that I could use my skills to create meaningful projects that would make a difference.
I'm the Co-founder of the Lowel AI project research, an AI device that uses microscopic cameras, neural networks and javascript TensonFlow.js to detect bacteria present in water. At Nusrat Senior Secondary, I'm the president of the ICT club. AI is my obssession.
When not coding, working on a research or working on tech related, I enjoy playing sport, spending time with family and eating too much chocolate. I currently resides in The Gambia.
